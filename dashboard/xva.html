Methodology

CVA — Credit Valuation Adjustment via Monte Carlo EPE. Exposure simulated under risk-neutral measure; CDS spreads used for PD calibration.


FVA — Funding Valuation Adjustment. Symmetric FVA using OIS–LIBOR basis applied to uncollateralised exposures. Netting sets with full CSA excluded.


PFE — Potential Future Exposure at 97.5th percentile. Used for limit monitoring and stress. MPOR-adjusted EE reported separately.


Source — pyxva engine when available; static demo fallback otherwise. Stress multipliers are applied analytically to baseline profiles.
