Why This Business Matters

global-bank realism

Securities finance is hidden infrastructure

Repo and stock loan are the financing rails underneath rates, credit, equities, and hedge-fund flow. Without them, a dealer balance sheet is incomplete.

Funding and margin are product features

The edge is not just inventory. It is term, haircuts, specials pricing, and how quickly the firm can reprice financing when risk changes.

Prime brokerage ties the bank together

Prime financing links markets, treasury, counterparty risk, collateral, and compliance. It is one of the fastest ways to make Apex feel like a global house.
